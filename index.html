What We Do

Commercial Fencing Installation & Material Sales

We specialize exclusively in commercial fencing (no residential work). From full-scale installation to material-only sales, we serve contractors, municipalities, and businesses across Kansas City.

Commercial Only

Chain Link Fencing

Heavy-duty chain link for job sites, industrial facilities, schools, parks, and secure perimeters. Available in multiple gauges and heights to meet any project spec.

Commercial Only

Wood Fencing

High-quality wood fencing for commercial properties, housing developments, and large-scale projects. Built for durability and a clean, professional appearance.

Commercial Only

Metal & Ornamental Fencing

Wrought iron, steel, and ornamental metal fencing for businesses, athletic facilities, and government projects. Strong, secure, and built to last.

Material-Only Sales

Need materials without installation? We sell commercial-grade fencing materials directly to contractors and businesses. New materials only — no salvage or refurbished product. Reach out to discuss what you need.
